Privacy Policy

Arkanolf respects your privacy. This policy transparently describes how the game handles — or rather, does not handle — your data.

1. Data Collection

Arkanolf does not collect any personal data. There are no sign-up forms, and we never request your name, email address, or any other information.

2. Cookies and Tracking

Arkanolf does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools of any kind. No browsing data is collected or transmitted to third parties.

3. Local Storage

The game may use your browser's localStorage to save local preferences (such as sound or difficulty settings). This data stays exclusively on your device and is never sent to any server.

4. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to privacy@arkanolf.com.
